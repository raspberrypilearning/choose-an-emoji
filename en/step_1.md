### The emoji keyboard

Your device might have an emoji keyboard that you can use to select an emoji. 

+ **Mobile or tablet**: Press the emoji icon (this is typically a smiley face)
+ **Windows**: Windows key + Full stop
+ **Mac**: CTRL + CMD + Space
+ **Linux**: Ctrl + Alt + E

### Emojipedia

If you can't find what you are looking for in the examples above then you can visit [emojipedia](https://emojipedia.org/){:target="_blank"} and explore a wider variety there.
